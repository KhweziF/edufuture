About Us

Bridging the Educational Divide

EduFuture South Africa is a registered non-profit organisation committed to breaking the cycle of poverty through education. We believe that every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, deserves access to quality learning resources and supportive mentorship to reach their full potential. Founded in 2020, we work directly with schools and communities in townships and rural areas to create lasting change.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: A South Africa where all youth have equal opportunity to thrive in the digital economy.

Mission: To bridge the digital divide in South African education by providing technology access, digital skills training, and educational support to students in disadvantaged communities.

Our Programs

  • Digital Literacy Labs

    We establish computer labs in partner schools and provide structured curriculum teaching basic computer skills, internet safety, and productivity software.

  • Coding for Youth

    After-school programs introducing programming concepts through visual coding platforms, building problem-solving skills and logical thinking.

  • Teacher Training

    Professional development workshops helping educators integrate technology into their classrooms effectively.

How We Empower Learners

Our programmes are designed to provide holistic support, addressing key barriers to education.

  • Digital Literacy Hubs

    We establish and equip computer labs, providing vital 21st-century skills and internet access for learning.

  • After-School Tutoring & Mentorship

    University student volunteers provide academic support and positive role modelling in key subjects like Maths, Science, and English.

  • Scholar Support Packs

    We distribute essential supplies including school uniforms, textbooks, stationery, and sanitary products to remove financial burdens from families.

  • Career Guidance Workshops

    We connect high school learners with professionals to explore future study and career pathways.
